History of Chu Concert Grounds

Chu Concert Grounds was established to create a space where music lovers can experience unforgettable performances. Founded by Liralyn P. Chu, it serves as a hub for artists, fans, and music enthusiasts to come together and celebrate the power of live music.

About the Founder

Liralyn Chu is a passionate young visionary who founded Chu Concert Grounds to provide a venue for extraordinary musical experiences. Currently studying at Arellano University Andres Bonifacio Campus as a Grade 11 ICT student, she is dedicated to promoting live entertainment and supporting artists.
